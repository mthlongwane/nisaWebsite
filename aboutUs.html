Nisa Finance - Our Why?

We believe that small businesses are the best way in which we can grow the economy and decrease unemployment.
We also believe that the engine that will unlock this growth will be through revolutionising cash-flow financing for SMEs.

@Nisa Finance - 2018


Meet The Founders

Founded in 2016 out of the University of Cape Town, NISA Finance has at its core, the mission to help SMEs experience sustainable growth to create employment.
Our goal is to one day have Stokvels financing these SMEs, we are going to reshape the financial services sector and bring about financial inclusion.
